Marinated Steak Recipe

Description

This recipe is for the best steak marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 pinch salt to taste
  • 1 pinch pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, dijon mustard, and garlic in a small bowl
  2. Season with salt and pepper
