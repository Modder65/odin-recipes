Grilled Salmon Recipe

Description

This recipe is for a simple grilled salmon served with white rice and broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds salmon fillets
  • lemon pepper to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt
  2. In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved
  3. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  5. Preheat grill for medium heat
  6. Lightly oil grill grate. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade
  7. Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork
